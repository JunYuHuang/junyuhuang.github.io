Our Journey

Eight years ago, we started our pen-distributing business in a college dorm in Richmond, British Columbia as a sort of a fun side project in our free time while we were working part time in a restaurant.

It wasn't much, but eventually a couple of local artists and illustrators caught wind of our specialty pen "shop" and then the project expanded into what we now: Penzaar, a national Canadian company, dedicated to distributing the most high quality pens to professionals who need them.

And in a nutshell, that is how we got here. We are confident that we can help you make you a success story, one quality pen at a time.
